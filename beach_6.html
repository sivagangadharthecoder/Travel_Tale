Plage du Sillon

Name: Plage du Sillon

Why it is called so: "Plage du Sillon" translates to "Beach of the Furrow," referring to the distinctive furrows in the sand.

Location: Saint-Malo, Brittany, France

Famous for: Long sandy beach, historic walled city, and significant tidal variations.

Length: Approximately 3 km

Area: Covers the Saint-Malo coastal area.

Geographical Location: Northern coast of Brittany.

Rating: 4.6/5
