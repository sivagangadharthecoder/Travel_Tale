Cascade de Rossignolet is a small waterfall near the village of Mont Dore in the region Auvergne/Puy de Dome, France. A few hundred meters further there is an exit to “les cascades” and a camping, go right here.This is the trailhead to Cascade de Rossignolet and Cascade de Queureuilh. Take the trail that runs west along the cemetery. The walk takes about 15-20 minutes and is easy to do.
