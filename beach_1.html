Nice Beach (Promenade des Anglais)

Name: Nice Beach (Promenade des Anglais)

Why it is called so: Named after the English aristocrats who funded the construction of the promenade in the 19th century.

Location: Nice, French Riviera, France

Famous for: Iconic seaside promenade, historical significance, and vibrant cultural scene.

Length: Approximately 7 km

Area: Covers a significant part of the Nice coastline.

Geographical Location: Mediterranean coast of southeastern France.

Rating: 4.5/5
