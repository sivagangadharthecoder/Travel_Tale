Musée d'Orsay
The Musée d'Orsay, housed in a former Beaux-Arts railway station, captivates with its unique blend of history and art. Within its luminous halls, masterpieces of Impressionism and Post-Impressionism come to life, showcasing the genius of artists like Monet, Van Gogh, and Degas. The grand clock watches over visitors as they journey through time, exploring a collection that spans the late 19th to early 20th centuries.
