About
Jardin du Luxembourg in Paris's 6th arrondissement is a beautiful and historic park surrounding the Luxembourg Palace. Created in 1612 by Marie de' Medici, the park features meticulously maintained lawns, tree-lined promenades, and the famous Medici Fountain, a picturesque spot for relaxation. The gardens are known for their stunning flowerbeds, statues, and the large octagonal Grand Bassin pond where children sail model boats.
