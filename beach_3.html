Palombaggia Beach

Name: Palombaggia Beach

Why it is called so: Named after the local area of Palombaggia in Corsica.

Location: Porto-Vecchio, Corsica, France

Famous for: Crystal clear turquoise waters, white sand, and scenic pine trees.

Length: Approximately 2 km

Area: Encompasses a stretch of the Porto-Vecchio coastline.

Geographical Location: Southeastern coast of Corsica.

Rating: 4.8/5
