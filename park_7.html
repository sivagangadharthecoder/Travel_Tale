Parc National de la Vanoise
located in the French Alps within the Savoie region, is named after the Vanoise mountain range. Famous for its breathtaking alpine landscapes, the park is home to diverse wildlife such as ibex and chamois. With an extensive network of trails, it offers ample opportunities for hiking, skiing, and mountaineering. Spanning 535 square kilometers, it's a premier destination for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts.
