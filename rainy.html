Paris
Paris. Home to the world-famous Eiffel Tower, Paris is undoubtedly one of the best places in France in December to visit with your loved ones.
Provence
Provence, a region in southeastern France bordering Italy and the Mediterranean Sea, is known for its diverse landscapes
Lyon
Lyon, the capital city in France’s Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes region, sits at the junction of the Rhône and Saône rivers.
Bordeaux
Bordeaux, hub of the famed wine-growing region, is a port city on the Garonne River in southwestern France.
Menton
Menton is a town on the French Riviera in southeast France. It’s known for beaches and gardens such as the Serre de la Madone garden, showcasing rare plants.
